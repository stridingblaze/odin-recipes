Pastelon

Description

Pastelon is a lasagna made with plantains. Serve with plain white rice and a salad.

Ingredients

  • 5 or 6 ripe plantains, peeled
  • 4 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt, plus more to taste
  • 1 large pot of sliced green beans (green bean salad)
  • 2 pounds of ground beef
  • 1/4 cup of vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons of sofrito (made of blended green pepper, onion and garlic)
  • 2 tablespoons of tomate paste
  • 1 packet of Sazon seasoning with coriander and annatto
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 2 tablespoons of stuffed olives

Steps for preparation

  1. Slice the plantains lengthwise.
  2. Fry plantain slices in a pan with plenty of oil until they are golden brown on both sides.
  3. Drain the fried plantains on paper towels and set to the side.
  4. In another skillet over medium heat, add the oil, sofrito, tomato paste, Sazon and cumin and cook for 2 minutes.
  5. Add the ground beef and cook for 10-15 minutes.
  6. Add the olives and mix together. Set to the side.
  7. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  8. Grease a rectangular or square pan with butter, pour in half of the beaten eggs.
  9. Place a layer of plantains, a layer of meat and then a layer of green beans.
  10. Cover with another layer of plantains, another layer of meat and green beans and continue layering. The last (top) layer should be plantains.
  11. Pour the rest of beaten eggs over the top.
  12. Bake for 30 minutes.
  13. Let stand for at least 15 minutes before serving.
